April 12, 2025

Finance Lesson

Teaching kids about money management through interactive games

We taught the kids about understanding important financial needs, like primary needs, secondary needs, clothing, and food needs. After the explanation, we jumped straight into practice. We had prepared engaging activities such as play shopping, where the kids could have a shopping simulation for their needs.

March 22, 2025

Counting Money

Interactive money counting exercises for children

This time, we gave them story exercises in the form of questions about shopping. Based on the story and context, they had to answer the questions. These types of questions were harder to do because it doesn't only require counting skills but also reading and understanding.

March 15, 2025

Fun Math

Making arithmetic enjoyable for young learners

On this visit, our main material was about math. We divided the class into 2 groups. 1 group consisted of kindergarten kids and the other elementary kids. We introduced the little kids to numbers from 1 to 10 and also taught them about simple addition.

February 22, 2025

Teamwork

Group activities teaching collaboration skills

We started the session with a newspaper game, where each group got the chance to answer questions given to them as fast as they could. The challenge is, each group had to answer a question given by us correctly in order for the newspaper to remain big enough for everyone to stand on comfortably.
